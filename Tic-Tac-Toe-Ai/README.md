## 🧠 About Minimax Algorithm

The Minimax Algorithm is a recursive decision-making algorithm used in Artificial Intelligence and game theory.

### The AI:
- Maximizes its chances of winning
- Minimizes the player's chances of winning
- Evaluates all possible game outcomes
- Chooses the best optimal move

This makes the AI highly challenging and difficult to beat.

## 🎯 Game Rules

- The player uses **X**
- The AI uses **O**
- Click on any empty cell to make a move
- The first player to align 3 symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins
- If all cells are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw
